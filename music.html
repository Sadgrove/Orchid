Orchid 路 Cid Made Me Do It EP (Free Download)

Cid Made Me Do It - This EP was inspired by my first psychedelic experiences, hence the name. In it I try to capture the wonder and awe that opens up to you in such a experience. Especially in the first song 'OUH', I've played around with a vocal samples to create a haunting but hopefully awe inspiring melody. The next track 'Tangent' I've played around with detuned synth to set a obscure and slightly funky vibe, trying to address the obscurity that can appear in a trip. The last song 'Being' is a celebration of it's name! It's supposed to be joyous, fun, and feel like your floating through ethereal space.



Orchid 路 Asleep

Asleep - I wrote this song when living in a Zen temple. I think this piece reflects the yearning that I felt to wake up to my true nature. To wake up from our suffering and see the brightness of existence. That's why the song feels sluggish, because it's quite the slog to break free from our cloudy thoughts and into the clear mind.



Orchid 路 Milford ft. Charlotte Lloyd-Butler

Milford - Musically, this piece was inspired by Clams Casino's song 'Waterfalls' on the 'Rainforest' EP. I took the inspiration of the sounds bringing forth the essence of different elements. I had recently visited Milford Sound in New Zealand, and I was inspired by the magnificence of the mountains, so in this piece I try to capture their beauty, grandeur and majesty.



Orchid 路 Oisit

Oisit - This was one of my first compositions while at College. An ambient, atmospheric piece with slow progressing melody.



Aching - This was my first collaborative work. Charlotte Lloyd-Butler is a very talented singer-songwriter who had written this song Aching, originally for guitar. We altered the piece and Charlotte played the piano instead, we found that this worked better with the synths. I produced the rest over the top to create a track that jumps between being energetic and calm.
