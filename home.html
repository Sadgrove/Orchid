About me


To place Orchid in a musical genre, is like trying to stop a caterpillar from turning in to a beautiful butterfly. His ability to be inspired from a range of different sources ranging from classical to synth-wave to Buddhist chant and fusing them together he paves the way for new sounds and relationships to emerge adding something truly innovative to the world of music.
